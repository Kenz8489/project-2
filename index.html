Meta, formerly known as Facebook, is a global social media company founded by Mark Zuckerberg. It focuses on building technology to connect people, primarily through its flagship platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp. Meta is also pioneering advancements in virtual reality and the metaverse.
Amazon, founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994, is the largest e-commerce platform globally. Known initially as an online bookstore, Amazon has expanded into cloud computing, digital streaming, artificial intelligence, and more. Its diverse services include Amazon Prime, AWS (Amazon Web Services), and a vast marketplace for consumers worldwide.
Netflix is a global streaming giant that started as a DVD rental service in 1997. Today, it is a leading provider of entertainment content, offering a wide array of movies, TV series, and original programming. It has redefined how people consume media with its subscription-based model and award-winning original content.
Google, founded by Larry Page and Sergey Brin in 1998, is a technology company best known for its search engine. Over the years, Google has expanded into cloud computing, software, and hardware development, and operates services like YouTube, Google Maps, and Android. Its parent company, Alphabet Inc., oversees a variety of innovative ventures.
Adobe is a global leader in creative software, known for its industry-standard tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere Pro. Founded in 1982, Adobe has expanded into digital marketing and document management with products like Adobe Acrobat and Creative Cloud, enabling creatives and professionals to design, edit, and publish content across multiple platforms.
Spotify, launched in 2008, is one of the world’s largest music streaming services. With a massive catalog of songs, podcasts, and exclusive content, it allows users to stream, discover, and share music. Spotify's freemium model offers both ad-supported and premium subscription options, making it a dominant force in the music industry.
Founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in 1975, Microsoft is a technology giant best known for its Windows operating system and Office productivity suite. Today, it also excels in cloud computing through Azure, gaming with Xbox, and software development tools. Microsoft continues to innovate in AI, hardware, and enterprise solutions.
